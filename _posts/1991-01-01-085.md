**Benefits**

Due to the reduction of the emission of greenhouse gases, a given product or service is climate friendly, which makes it stand out in its product category. The label is awarded on the basis of the product life cycle analysis and comparison with other products.

**Where to find**

The label can be awarded to any group of products but most frequently you can find it on construction materials, cosmetics, textiles and electrical and electronic equipment.
